Who I am & What I aim to become

A self-directed path: growing up in the French suburbs and reaching top national scientific institutions through hard work and perseverance. Passionate about these values and culture in all its diversity, I have dreamed since childhood of intellectual, professional, and social advancement.

After five years of intense study, I am proud to hold the title of “Ingénieur”, with two master’s degrees. Still driven by my characteristic thirst to thrive, but now with greater maturity and backed by successful international experience, I am now ready to enter the professional world, or pursue studies, with conviction.

With a rigorous general engineering background in physics, biophysics, mathematics, chemistry and computer science; I hope to contribute to innovation that addresses the challenges of our world, particularly in areas such as population safety, resource management, or the development of a more environmentally responsible industry.
