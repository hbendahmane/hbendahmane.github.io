“Luctor et emergo”1

1. Luctor et emergo (“I struggle and I emerge”) is the historic motto of the Dutch province of Zeeland (16th century), symbolizing perseverance and resilience in the face of adversity. It is also the title of Ed Dean’s 2010 book Luctor et Emergo: Latin for I Struggle and Overcome, where the expression conveys resilience in life’s struggles and the pursuit of one’s goals.
