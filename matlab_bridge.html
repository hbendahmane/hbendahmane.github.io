MATLAB - Bridge Health Monitoring System

Goal

The purpose of the Bridge Health Monitoring System is to provide a comprehensive, real-time assessment of a bridge’s structural integrity. The code integrates sensor measurements and traffic loads to evaluate the bridge’s safety and predict remaining service life. 1

Engineering Approach and Tools

This MATLAB implementation uses a combination of finite element model updating, modal analysis, fatigue life assessment, and reliability-based safety evaluation.

Execution Behavior and Output Interpretation

For a representative scenario, the system outputs a Safety Index of 4.472, a Remaining Life of 190 years, and identifies one critical element.
